# Lumenora Home — Botanical Editorial Mockup

## Objective

Create one high-fidelity desktop, full-page image mockup for the existing Lumenora Home route. Rebuild the visual language and layout while preserving the Lumenora brand name and limiting the page to the existing global header, one hero, and the footer.

The mockup is a visual concept only. It must not add routes, pages, or navigation destinations that do not already exist in `Frontend/src/App.tsx`.

## Scope

- Canvas: desktop, 1440 px wide, full-page composition.
- Page sequence: Header → Hero → Footer.
- Existing destinations allowed in visible navigation: Home, Shop, Quiz, Blog, Wishlist, and Cart.
- Remove all Home sections between Hero and Footer, including the brand marquee, feature product, product grids, secondary banner, and newsletter.
- Do not depict Product, Checkout, Login, Profile, About, Contact, or any other unimplemented route.

## Creative Direction

Use an original “Botanical Editorial” direction informed by two references:

- Violet Grey: strong editorial hierarchy, concise labels, decisive contrast, and beauty imagery presented like a fashion publication.
- Aesop: restrained palette, deliberate whitespace, tactile natural materials, and calm typographic rhythm.

The result should be recognizably Lumenora, not a copy of either reference site.

## Visual System

- Parchment: `#E8E0D2`
- Ivory: `#F4F0E8`
- Charcoal: `#181713`
- Burgundy: `#6B1F2B`
- Olive: `#69705A`
- Display typography: high-contrast editorial serif.
- Utility typography: restrained modern grotesk/sans-serif in small uppercase labels.
- Geometry: squared editorial blocks and hairline rules; avoid rounded pill navigation and excessive card styling.

## Header

- Warm parchment background with a thin charcoal rule.
- Large centered `LUMENORA` wordmark with generous tracking.
- Existing primary links only: Home, Shop, Quiz, Blog.
- Existing utility actions only: Search, Wishlist, Cart.
- No pill-shaped menu container.
- Keep hierarchy compact so the hero remains the dominant element.

## Hero

- Asymmetric split composition, approximately 42% text and 58% imagery.
- Left panel: parchment/ivory field, small editorial eyebrow `BOTANICAL INTELLIGENCE`, oversized serif headline `Skin, considered.`, short supporting copy, primary CTA `SHOP THE EDIT`, and secondary CTA `TAKE THE SKIN QUIZ`.
- Right panel: original high-end botanical skincare editorial still life using amber glass, stone, botanical leaves, warm directional light, and controlled shadows.
- Imagery should feel tactile and premium without imitating specific Violet Grey or Aesop campaign assets.
- Use burgundy sparingly for a label, rule, or CTA emphasis; olive appears through botanical elements or a restrained color field.
- Maintain strong readable contrast and generous negative space.
- Do not add carousels, product grids, promotional modules, or content sections inside or below the hero.

## Footer

- Charcoal background with ivory typography and subtle olive/burgundy accents.
- Left area: Lumenora wordmark and existing brand statement.
- Navigation only to existing destinations: Shop, Quiz, Blog, Wishlist, and Cart.
- Include a compact copyright line.
- Do not show links to About, Contact, Sustainability, FAQ, Shipping, Account, Privacy, or other unimplemented pages.

## UX Intent

- Reduce the current page’s uneven density and long sequence of unrelated promotional sections.
- Give users one dominant visual message and two clear actions.
- Preserve orientation through a simple global header.
- Use the footer as a concise end-state rather than a directory of unavailable pages.
- Ensure labels, navigation, and CTA text remain legible at the rendered mockup scale.

## Mockup Constraints

- High-fidelity website UI mockup, not concept art or a device mockup.
- Straight-on desktop page view; no laptop frame, perspective tilt, hands, or surrounding scene.
- Render the exact brand text `LUMENORA`, eyebrow `BOTANICAL INTELLIGENCE`, headline `Skin, considered.`, and CTA labels `SHOP THE EDIT` and `TAKE THE SKIN QUIZ`.
- No third-party logos, trademarks, watermarks, or copied campaign imagery.
- No extra pages or sections.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The image visibly contains only Header, Hero, and Footer.
- All visible navigation destinations correspond to the six existing routes.
- The composition uses the approved Botanical Editorial palette and asymmetric hero.
- The page reads as a coherent Lumenora redesign influenced by Violet Grey and Aesop while remaining original.
- Text hierarchy and primary actions are readable.
